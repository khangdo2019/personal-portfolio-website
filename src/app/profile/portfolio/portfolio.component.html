Stiff Money App (Finastra Hackathon)

Timeline: Sep 2020 - Dec 2020

Technologies Used: Biconomy, Avalanche JS, Finastra API, React JS, Fusion Auth, Node JS, Google Cloud Service

Language: Javascript, HTML5, CSS3

Role: Back-end Developer

  1. Problem: Many individuals intend to invest in Real Estate with their savings and many First time Home Buyers struggle to get their dream home due to low credit limit.
  2. Challenge: Find a sufficient platform to invest free cashflow from depositors with a competitive ROI.
  3. Solution:
    1. Used Biconomy and Avalanche JS to manage the book, and on the other side, used Finastra API to manage the lending platform.
    2. Converted free cashflow to coin to get 10% APR to maintain the interest rate for lenders.
    3. Helped Real Estate Developers and First Time Home Buyers to borrow for Real Estate purchase at 12% and zero down payment.
